About Me

I am a Management Engineering student at the University of Waterloo with practical experience in software development, data analysis, and technical documentation across a range of industries. Originally from Trujillo, Peru, I bring an fresh perspective to problem-solving and innovation.

My technical skills include proficiency in Python, HTML, VBA, and SQL, with a strong foundation in database design and management. I am fluent in English and Spanish, and hold a DELF B1 certification in French, which supports my ability to work effectively in multicultural and multilingual environments.

I am passionate about building scalable, data-driven solutions that enhance systems, optimize processes, and improve user experiences.
